This Program is written to calculate the Body mass index(BMI).

Based on the BMI Index Number, the code will further provide the interpretation of as per standard BMI Tables.

Below are the interpretation of  BMI based on the BMI value.

Under 18.5 they are underweight
Over 18.5 but below 25 they have a normal weight
Over 25 but below 30 they are slightly overweight
Over 30 but below 35 they are obese
Above 35 they are clinically obese.
